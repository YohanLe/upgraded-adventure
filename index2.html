Introduction - Where I born and grow up

My Name is Yohannese , I am originally from East Africa, Ethiopia. Ethiopia is a nation with a very diverse culture. Ethiopia is the origin of human life and the first nation to introduce Coffee to the world.Ethiopia is a home for more than 80 nations and nationalities. I grew up drinking coffee since middle school. Drinking coffee in Ethiopia serves as one of the ceremonies where all community challenges and ideas are hashed out within the members of the community. Families gather to arrange a wedding ceremony and to mourn the loss of loved ones while having coffee.There is a specific way of making coffee, we first get the coffee bean, roast it, grind it and then boil it. To let the grinded coffee bean powder settle the coffee will let sit for a few minutes then what comes out is very testy and original fresh coffee.

  • Oromo
  • Amhara
  • Somaliya

A city of many regional and international companies has their headquarters and the fastest growing city. Addis is an old and under developed city, growing in a crowded city comes with many challenges like housing and transportation.
